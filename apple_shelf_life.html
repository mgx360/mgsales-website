Apple Shelf Life in Reefer Shipping

Back to Blog

When exporting apples overseas, understanding shelf life is critical. At MG SALES, we specialize in reefer container shipping of fresh apples to India and Asia. Proper timing and storage help preserve quality.

What Impacts Shelf Life?

  • Temperature consistency inside the reefer container
  • Total transit time (including delays)
  • Packaging type (e.g. 18 kg bushel or 13 kg wooden box)
  • Apple variety (Royal Gala vs. Red Delicious)

Expected Shelf Life Main Varieties

  • Royal Gala 30-45 days from packing, depending on cold chain
  • Red Delicious slightly longer due to firm skin
  • Red Jonaprince 20-30 days max, requires rapid turnover

Tips for Buyers

  • Request reefer container with data logger
  • Avoid weekend transits (delays at ports)
  • Ask for Proforma with estimated transit duration
  • Prioritize direct shipping lines (fewer transshipments)
